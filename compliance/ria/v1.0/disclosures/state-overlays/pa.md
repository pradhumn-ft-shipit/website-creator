# State overlay — Pennsylvania (PA)

**Applies when:** advisor is **state-registered** (AUM < $100M) with a primary state of Pennsylvania (§5.5).
State-registered investment advisers in Pennsylvania register with the **Pennsylvania Department of Banking and
Securities**.

## Footer registration line (replaces the generic state line)

> {{firm_name}} is registered as an investment adviser with the Pennsylvania Department of Banking and Securities.
> Registration does not imply a certain level of skill or training.
