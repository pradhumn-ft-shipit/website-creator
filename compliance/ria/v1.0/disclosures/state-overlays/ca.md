# State overlay — California (CA)

**Applies when:** advisor is **state-registered** (AUM < $100M) with a primary state of California (§5.5).
State-registered investment advisers in California register with the **California Department of Financial
Protection and Innovation (DFPI)**.

## Footer registration line (replaces the generic state line)

> {{firm_name}} is registered as an investment adviser with the California Department of Financial Protection
> and Innovation. Registration does not imply a certain level of skill or training.

## State-specific notes (verify with counsel before publish)

- Confirm current DFPI disclosure-placement and brochure-delivery requirements under the California Corporate
  Securities Law of 1968 / CCR Title 10.
- No state-specific *website* prohibitions beyond the federal set are encoded in v1.0; flag if review identifies any.
