# State overlay — Georgia (GA)

**Applies when:** advisor is **state-registered** (AUM < $100M) with a primary state of Georgia (§5.5).
State-registered investment advisers in Georgia register with the **Georgia Securities Division (Office of the
Secretary of State)**.

## Footer registration line (replaces the generic state line)

> {{firm_name}} is registered as an investment adviser with the Georgia Securities Division. Registration does
> not imply a certain level of skill or training.

## State-specific notes (verify with counsel before publish)

- Confirm current Georgia Securities Division disclosure rules under the Georgia Uniform Securities Act of 2008
