# State overlay — New York (NY)

**Applies when:** advisor is **state-registered** (AUM < $100M) with a primary state of New York (§5.5).
Investment adviser registration in New York is administered by the **New York State Department of Law (Office
of the Attorney General), Investor Protection Bureau** under the Martin Act.

## Footer registration line (replaces the generic state line)

> {{firm_name}} is registered as an investment adviser with the New York State Department of Law, Investor
> Protection Bureau. Registration does not imply a certain level of skill or training.

## State-specific notes (verify with counsel before publish)

- New York's registration regime under the Martin Act (General Business Law Article 23-A) differs from the model
  state regime; confirm current IA disclosure requirements with counsel.
- No state-specific *website* prohibitions beyond the federal set are encoded in v1.0; flag if review identifies any.
